_1994. A designer and developer focusing on interfaces and interactions. Working remotely._  
_Get in touch via [Twitter](https://twitter.com/shuding\_) / [g@shud.in](mailto:g@shud.in)._

_Started programming in high school and has since spent 4 years on [algorithm competitions](#awards--honors), 
Shu worked as a [graphic designer and web developer](#web-design--development) in Shanghai, cofounded a tech startup company, and got his BSc degree in Computer Science from Fudan University. He created a lot of [game and HCI projects](#hci--game-design), and taught himself drawing, photography and guitar._

_With wide range of interests from engineering to art, Shu always see “Be curious. [Read widely](#bibliography-design-and-social-links). Try new things.”
(by [Aaron Swartz](http://aaronsw.com)) as the guide to [creativity](https://spectrum.mit.edu/winter-2017/neri-oxmans-krebs-cycle-of-creativity)._
